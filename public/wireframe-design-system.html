Typography Scale

Two font families, nine levels. Inter (font-sans) for readable prose & headings. JetBrains Mono (font-mono) for code, labels, badges, and terminal chrome.

Font Convention:
font-mono — Terminal chrome: labels, badges, buttons, metadata, footer, sidebar, prompts
font-sans — Readable text: headings, descriptions, body content, card text
font-sans on h1–h3 — mandatory. Headings read better in sans-serif.
